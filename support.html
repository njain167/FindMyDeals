Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install FindMyDeals?

Installing FindMyDeals is quick and easy:

  • Visit the Chrome Web Store (link coming soon)
  • Click "Add to Chrome"
  • Confirm the installation when prompted
  • The extension will appear in your Chrome toolbar
  • Click on the extension icon to get started
How does FindMyDeals work?

FindMyDeals uses advanced AI to scan your Gmail promotional emails and extract deals, discounts, and vouchers. Here's how it works:

  • You grant permission to scan your Gmail promotional folder
  • Our secure API processes your emails to identify deals
  • Extracted deals are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
  • You can access all your deals through the extension popup or Gmail integration
  • Your email content is never stored on our servers
Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! Privacy is our top priority:

  • We never store your emails on our servers
  • Email content is processed securely through our API and immediately discarded
  • Only extracted deal information is stored locally on your device
  • We don't sell, share, or monetize your personal data
  • All data processing happens with end-to-end encryption

For complete details, please read our Privacy Policy.

The extension isn't finding my deals. What should I do?

If FindMyDeals isn't discovering deals, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Make sure you've granted Gmail permissions to the extension
  • Check that you have promotional emails in your Gmail account
  • Ensure you're connected to the internet during scanning
  • Try refreshing your Gmail page and restarting the extension
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reinstall the extension

If problems persist, please contact our support team.

Can I use FindMyDeals on mobile devices?

Currently, FindMyDeals is available as a Chrome extension for desktop and laptop computers. We're working on mobile compatibility and will announce updates when available. In the meantime, you can access your discovered deals through Gmail on any device where they're stored locally.

How do I update or uninstall FindMyDeals?

To update: Chrome extensions update automatically. You can manually check for updates in Chrome Settings > Extensions.

To uninstall:

  • Right-click the FindMyDeals icon in your Chrome toolbar
  • Select "Remove from Chrome"
  • Confirm the removal
  • Your locally stored deals will be removed with the extension
What types of deals can FindMyDeals discover?

FindMyDeals can identify various types of savings in your emails:

  • Discount codes and coupon codes
  • Percentage-off deals (10% off, 25% off, etc.)
  • Dollar-amount discounts ($5 off, $20 off, etc.)
  • Free shipping offers
  • Gift cards and store credits
  • Buy-one-get-one (BOGO) deals
  • Limited-time sales and flash deals
  • Cashback offers
