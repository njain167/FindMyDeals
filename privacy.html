Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At FindMyDeals, privacy isn't just a policy - it's our foundation. We've built our service with your privacy as our top priority, ensuring your data remains under your control at all times.

How FindMyDeals Works

  • Local Processing: All email scanning and deal detection happens directly on your device. Your emails never leave your browser.
  • No Data Storage: We don't store your emails, login credentials, or personal information on our servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect your data, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Email contents or attachments
  • Login credentials
  • Personal identification information
  • Banking or financial information

Limited Information We Collect

We collect minimal anonymous usage statistics to improve our service:

  • Extension installation and uninstallation events
  • Basic error reports
  • Anonymous feature usage statistics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without creating an account
  • Delete all locally stored data at any time
  • Opt-out of anonymous usage statistics
